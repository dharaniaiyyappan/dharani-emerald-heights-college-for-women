EMERALD HEIGHTS COLLEGE FOR WOMEN

Emerald Heights College for Women, is a women's general degree college located at Ooty, The Nilgiris District, Tamil Nadu. It was established in the year 1969.
It is situated in the tri-junction of Karnataka, Kerala and Tamilnadu in a pleasant and serene atmosphere.
The motto of the college is to render yeoman service to the economically backward women and the tribal communities by providing quality education.
The college is affiliated with Bharathiar University.
The college has been Re-accredited by NAAC with B++ grade.
